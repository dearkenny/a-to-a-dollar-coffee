Protocol & Vision

Body: Lora (Regular)
A2A is an experimental framework for machine-to-machine value transfer. Agents negotiate autonomously, settle through x402 payments, and complete tasks without human intermediaries. Serif body text adds warmth and readability to longer passages, balancing the technical rigor of monospace headlines.

Design Foundation

Spacing

8px base unit. Comfortable density with breathing room. Spacing scale: xs(4) sm(8) md(16) lg(24) xl(32) 2xl(48)

Border Radius

Subtle: sm(2px) md(4px) lg(6px). Sharp edges preserve geek aesthetic without harshness.

Layout

Editorial grid with asymmetry. 12-column on desktop, flexible on mobile. Max content width: 1200px

Motion

Minimal-functional only. Transitions aid comprehension: 150ms ease-in-out. No gratuitous animations.
